Economy

Lethbridge is the biggest city south of Calgary. Lethbridge's strategical location provides an economic outpost for neighboring cities and communities.

Those in other cities within Southern Alberta that look to pursue post-secondary education tend to turn towards either Lethbridge College or the University of Lethbridge. Both the college and university are considered to be among the best in the entire country. These post-secondary institutions offer some of the highest quality education in Canada.

Much of the economy used to be based on agriculture, but that has since changed to include many other professions that also contribute to economic growth. Lethbridge averages a GDP of $5.8B annually.
