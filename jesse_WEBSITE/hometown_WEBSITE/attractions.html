Attractions

Lethbridge has a hockey team known as the Lethbridge Hurricanes that play in the Western Hockey League. The ENMAX Centre is a 5,900-seat entertainment venue that hosts a variety of different attractions. This includes hockey, concerts, skating, basketball, wrestling, and more.

Lethbridge is known for its mountainous terrain, often referred to as the “coulees.” There are plenty of trails along the interior of the coulees that you can hike or bike through.

The city has several membership institutions. One of the biggest ones is the YMCA, which offers a gym, swimming pool, and a full sports gymnasium with various activities all for a monthly price.

Lethbridge has a nature reserve, golf course, park, museum, gardens, and much more that will make your visit special!
