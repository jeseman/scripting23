People

The majority of the people that reside in Lethbridge are of European descent. However, the city is home to a pretty sizeable minority of Indigenous peoples.

Lethbridge is home to the Blackfoot Confederacy and was once home to Indigenous Peoples. Most of Southern Alberta is under Treaty 7, including Lethbridge.

Lethbridge was the fourth city in the province of Alberta to climb above 100,000 residents. According to the Government of Alberta, the population of Lethbridge increases roughly 2.36% each year, and has increased by 8.02% within the past five years.

50% of the citizens in Lethbridge are Christian. However, that statistic has been declining in recent years. 84% of Lethbridge's citizens speak English.
