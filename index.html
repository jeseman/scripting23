About

Jesse is an individual who creates content for various social media platforms and specializes in video creation and thumbnail design. He started creating content back in 2016 when he was just 11 years old. He has seen success in various different fields of content creation; such as video creation, short videos, and live streams. His content primarily revolves around Nintendo and other things gaming.

His channel was monetized from April 2020 to June 2022, in which it brought in thousands of of dollars over the course of those two years. His ultimate goal is to find a full time career in the New Media field while making his channel a side hustle.

Biography

One of his favourite things to do outside of content creation is watching and playing hockey, with his favourite team being the Montreal Canadiens of the National Hockey League. He has been to the Bell Centre for games on numerous occasions. He recalls the atmosphere in the building as “electric”.

He documented his experiences in Montreal in several different series on his channel. The most prominent one being 'Montreal 2020', which shows many of the attractions that the city has to offer. He primarily showcased the atmosphere of the Bell Centre during regular season Habs games. The goal of the series was to show the naturality of the city to those who have not been able to experience the sites of Montreal for themselves.
